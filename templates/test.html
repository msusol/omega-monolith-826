Frequently Asked Questions

Who would get the most out of the class and hackathon?

Someone interested in machine learning. Ideally you are comfortable working with data and Dremel. There is no coding knowledge required.

What tools will we be using in the class?

We'll be using CoLabratory (go/colab) to explore data and LPH (go/lph) to build, tune and evaluate ML models.
